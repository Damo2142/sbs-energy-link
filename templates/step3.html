🔌 RS485 Port — /dev/ttyRS485
The RS485 port can be used for Modbus RTU (read additional serial devices) or BACnet MSTP (serve BACnet points to legacy BAS on RS485 trunk). Only one mode can be active at a time.
Add Modbus RTU devices on the RS485 trunk. Each device needs a unique address and a device profile. Points from RTU devices appear as additional BACnet objects in Device 9001.
